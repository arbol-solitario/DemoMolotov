Controls:

  • W,A,S,D: Move
  • Mouse Right Click: Shoot
  • Space: Dash
  • E: Interact (cars or objects on the floor)
  • P: Pause

Objective:

Try to fill as much as you can your car (orange) gas tank with the other cars gas. 4 types of diferent enemys will try to kill you. Shoot them before you die.
